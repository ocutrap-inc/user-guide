# Wi-Fi Setup Guide

Follow these steps to configure your OcuTrap device to connect to a Wi-Fi network.

**Step 1: Access the OcuTrap Device Management Interface**

1. **Open the OcuTrap Management Interface** on your computer or smartphone.
2. **Select Your Trap**&#x20;

**Step 2: Open the Settings Menu**

1. **Click on the "Settings" Button** in the trap management panel.

**Step 3: Configure Wi-Fi Settings**

1. **Enter the Wi-Fi Network SSID** in the "Wi-Fi Name" field.
2. **Enter the Wi-Fi Network Password** in the "Password" field.
3. **Select the Wi-Fi Authentication Type if needed** from the "Advanced" dropdown (WPA2, WPA, or WEP). This will default to WPA2 which is the standard for most Wi-Fi networks.
4. **Save the Settings** by clicking the "Save" button.

**Step 4: Verify the Connection**

1. **Restart your OcuTrap device** to apply the new settings.
2. **Check the Connection Status** in the OcuTrap management interface.

**Troubleshooting Tips**

* **Double-check SSID and password.**
* **Ensure Wi-Fi network is operational.**
* **Verify the authentication type matches your network.**

When enabled, OcuTrap will try to connect to Wi-Fi. If it has trouble connecting, it will fall back to cellular.
