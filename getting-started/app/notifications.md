# Notifications

### Notification Settings

Notifications can be configured for various events related to actions, errors, and captures. By default, a push notification is sent to the trap owner and any designated trap managers. However, these settings can be customized in the following ways:

* **Reminder Notifications**: Send another notification at a specified time after an event, e.g., a reminder 6 hours after a capture.
* **Email Notifications**: In addition to push notifications, users can opt to receive notifications via email.
* **Event-Specific Notifications**: Set up notifications for specific events, such as when the trap opens or closes, or errors.
