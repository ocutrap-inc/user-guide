# Hardware set-up

We call the plastic case containing the camera, circuitboard, and battery the "Pod".

* **Unpack the Components** - Carefully remove the OcuTrap from packaging.
* [support.md](../support/support.md "mention") if anything appears to be damaged.
* **Fully charge blue battery** with the accompanying charger in the small white box.
*   **Motor Assembly**: The motor attaches at two points, the hinge bracket and the door bracket.

    * Each top bracket and door bracket have a pin and clevis

**POD Assembly Instructions**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-09-10 at 8.09.31 PM.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Connect motor to top and bottom door hinge with pin and clevis
2. Feed motor cable through handle
3. Slide Pod down the back rails
4. Lock the Pod to the trap with the clip
5. Connect motor to the motor connector
