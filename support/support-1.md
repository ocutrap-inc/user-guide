# Bug reporting

If you find a bug, please report it quickly at our portal: [base.ocutrap.com/bug\_report](https://base.ocutrap.com/bug_report). Include a detailed bug description, reproduction steps, and any relevant visuals. Explain what you expected to happen and what actually did. Your detailed feedback helps improve our service.

You can also email [info@ocutrap.com](mailto:base.ocutrap.com/bug_report)
